How to cook Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Leftover Rice

    •

  • Vegetable Oil

    •

  • 3 Eggs

    •

  • 3 chillies

    •

  • 1 tablespoon of sambal

    •

  • 1 tablespoon of sweet soy

    •

  • teaspoon of galangal

    •

  • 1 bunch of chive blossoms

    •

  • 1 tablespoon of rendang

    •

Preparation:

  1. Chop the 3 chillies.

    2.

  2. Grate about 1 teaspoon of galangal onto the chopped chillies. The idea is that both the
    grated galangal and its juices create a paste and mix with the chillies for a sweeter and milder spice.

    3.

  3. Chop the chive blossoms and mix with the chillies and galangal paste.

    4.

  4. In a small bowl, crack and beat three eggs until evenly combined.

    5.

  5. Heat oil in a wok, spreading around the edges of the wok to fully cover and protect the pan. Throw out the excess oil
    and with a rag, carefully finish spreading the oil alongside the inside of the pan, so “it’s nice and sticky."

    6.

  6. Pour fresh oil into the wok.

    7.

  7. Add the chillies, galangal and chive blossoms into the wok and cook until softened.

    8.

  8. Lightly season with salt and pepper.

    9.

  9. Pour in beaten eggs and continuously whisk to break up eggs clumps. Alternate whisking with
    letting the eggs set until eggs are fully cooked and scrambled.

    10.

  10. (Optional) Move eggs and chillies mixture to a second, larger, wok.

    11.

  11. Add the leftover rice. On high heat, spread the rice onto all parts of the wok to ensure it all fries.

    12.

  12. Make a circle in the middle of the wok, pushing the rice up its sides. Add the sambal and rendang paste.
    Fry and then mix with rice.

    13.

  13. Add any left-over proteins, whether it be chicken, beef, fish, pork, or tofu and mix.

    14.

  14. Add 1 tablespoon of sweet soy.

    15.

  15. Keeping heat underneath the wok, toss to prevent the rice from sticking to the sides of the wok.

    16.

  16. Adjust seasoning to taste, toss it a couple more times, and serve!

    17.
